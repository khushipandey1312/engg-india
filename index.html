ABOUT US

Engineering India is a central club dedicated to leveraging the collective power of engineers and technology enthusiasts for the betterment of society.
Engineering India serves as a platform for collaboration,knowledge sharing, and networking
among like-minded individuals who share a common goal By harnessing the passion and talents
of its members, Engineering India is driving positive change, making
a lasting impact,and contributing to the betterment of society on both local and national scales.
